The Best Movies According to Nour

My Favourite Movies of all Time.

12 Angry Men (1957)

A movie about 12 jurors locked in a room to delibrate on the fate of a young man accused of stabbing his father.
The movie is full of suspense and great character growth with great performances from the entire cast and a great premise and keeps you engaged for its 95 minute runtime.

Tarzan (1999)

This is just for the nostalgic effect for me but the story is great the music is outstanding courtesy of the great phill collins and just a great time.

The Dark Knight (2008)

Outstanding performance by everyone involved especially Heath Ledger as the joker. Great cinematography and use of practical effects. It is just a bit too long (154 minutes) but overall defintely worth a watch.

Inception (2010)

One of the best Sci-Fi movies of all time with Christofer Nolan proving once again why he's a fantastic director.
You get what you're used to from any Nolan film: great acting, great cinematography, great use of practical effects.

Interstellar (2014)

Another one of Christofer Nolan's hits with all the usual suspects in terms of what a Nolan film provides you with the added bonus of a great and sometimes heart-wrenching performance by Mathew McConaughey.
